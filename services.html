Services Overview

Care of the aged

We understand the needs of our senior citizens. Our facility provides compassionate support to help older adults improve their quality of life.

Child delivery

Our maternity provides support and guidance throughout your childbirth journey. We are here to ensure a safe delivery experience for you and baby.

Baby immunization and monitoring

Our babies get the best start in life. We provide vaccinations that ensures our children receive the protection and support they need to thrive.

Reproductive health and cervical screening

We are dedicated to supporting women's health at every stage. We provide care, education, and treatment options to promote healthy reproductive well-being.

Counceling and family planning

We offer services and counceling on fertility guidance contraceptives to help you make informed decisions about your reproductive health.

General health and awareness

From check-ups and screenings to health workshops and counseling, we provide services that focus on prevention, education, and early detection, which you need to take control of your health and well-being.
